About the Hackathon

What is Blue Ventures Hackathon?

Get ready for the first-ever Blue Venture Hackathon coming this fall! This isn't just a weekend sprint; teams will dedicate seven days to research, develop, and build innovative solutions within their chosen track. You'll work side-by-side with experienced industry mentors, who will help bring your team from concept to product.

Ready to build, learn, grow, and have fun?

This hackathon welcomes both experienced coders and those eager to jump in. It's a unique opportunity to dive into the newest tech, learn directly from industry mentors, and create something great from scratch. Bring that idea you've always wanted to try and see it become a reality!

F.A.Q

What is a hackathon?

A hackathon is a collaborative event where teams of developers, designers, and entrepreneurs come together to build innovative projects in a limited time frame. It's a great opportunity to learn new skills, meet like-minded people, and bring your creative ideas to life!

Who can participate?

Our hackathon is open to students of all skill levels from high school to graduate school. Whether you're a complete beginner or an experienced developer, there's a place for you! We welcome participants from all backgrounds and disciplines.

Do I need a team?

Not at all! You can participate solo, come with a pre-formed team, or find teammates once you arrive. We'll help match you with others who share similar interests or complementary skills.

What should I bring?

Bring your laptop, charger, any hardware you want to work with, and a learner's attitude! We'll provide the coffee, meals, snacks, drinks, and swag.

Is there a cost to participate?

The participation fee is $50. We cover meals, snacks, swag, and all event activities. Please contact us if you'd like to participate, but cost is an issue.

What kind of projects can I build?

Anything you can imagine! Projects can be web apps, mobile apps, hardware hacks, games, AI/ML projects, or even music and poetry. We'll have several tracks and sponsor challenges with specific themes, but creativity is encouraged.

What if I'm a beginner?

Perfect! Hackathons are great learning experiences. We'll have mentors available throughout the event, beginner-friendly workshops, and plenty of resources to help you get started.

What are the prizes?

We have prizes for overall winners, track-specific categories, sponsor challenges, and special recognition awards. Prizes include cash awards, tech gadgets, internship opportunities, and exclusive swag. But remember, the real prize is the experience and connections you'll make!
